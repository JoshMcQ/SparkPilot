Runtime tradeoffs

When EMR Serverless is right, and when EMR on EKS is better

EMR Serverless is strong for low-frequency and sandbox use. SparkPilot focuses on governed batch operations where platform controls and operational consistency matter.

Serverless strengths

Lower ops burden and no persistent cluster management for ad-hoc workloads.

EKS + SparkPilot strengths

Governance controls, richer placement and queue policy options, and tighter operator workflows.

Current SparkPilot focus

Batch-first on EMR on EKS, with broader runtime operations planned next.
